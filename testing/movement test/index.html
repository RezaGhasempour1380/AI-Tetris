Tetris Movement Tests

Guide: Which Piece to Test can also be chosen

Test 1: Move Right

Guide: Press either Button "D" , "d" , "Right Arrow Key" for right movement

Purpose: To verify that the current Tetris piece moves to the right correctly when the key is pressed.

Test 2: Move Left

Guide: Press either Button "A" , "a" , "Left Arrow Key" for left movement

Purpose: To verify that the current Tetris piece moves to the left correctly when the key is pressed.

Test 3: Fast Downward Movement

Guide: Press either Button "S" , "s" , "Down Arrow Key" for fast going down movement

Purpose: To verify that the current Tetris piece moves down quickly when the key is pressed.
